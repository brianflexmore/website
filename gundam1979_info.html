Storyline

In Universal Century 0079, the Principality of Zeon declares independence from the Earth Federation, launching the One Year War. Zeon quickly gains the upper hand with their revolutionary humanoid weapons, the mobile suits.


The Earth Federation secretly develops its own mobile suit prototypes, including the RX-78-2 Gundam, at the neutral Side 7 colony. When Zeon's ace pilot, Char Aznable, discovers this, his reconnaissance attack on Side 7 kills most of the colony's inhabitants.


In the chaos, teenage mechanic Amuro Ray stumbles upon the Gundam and pilots it successfully, fending off the attack. He and other survivors are forced to join the crew of the assault carrier White Base. Now thrust into the war, Amuro must learn to fight for survival as the White Base crew, largely civilians, attempts to make their perilous journey to safety, all while being relentlessly pursued by Char Aznable.

Nations At War

Earth Federation

The Earth Federation is the governing body of Earth and its space colonies. In the One Year War, they represent the established authority fighting against Zeon's bid for independence.


The Federation fields advanced warships, experimental mobile suits like the Gundam, and relies on the White Base and its young crew to carry critical hopes for survival.

Principality of Zeon

The Principality of Zeon is a breakaway nation of space colonies that declared independence from the Earth Federation. Led by the ruling Zabi family, Zeon launched the One Year War seeking dominance over Earth and space.


Known for their powerful mobile suits and charismatic leaders, Zeon characters often embody ambition and militarism.

Main Characters

Earth Federation

Amuro Ray

Gundam Pilot

A teenage civilian who becomes the reluctant pilot of the Federation's prototype Gundam. His journey embodies ordinary people burdened with extraordinary responsibility.

Bright Noa

White Base Captain

A young officer thrust into command after senior crew are killed. Though strict and often clashing with Amuro, he matures into a reliable leader crucial to White Base's survival.

Sayla Mass

G-Fighter Pilot

A medical student turned soldier who pilots the G-Fighter support craft. Secretly Char's sister, adding emotional complexity and divided loyalties to her role.

Kai Shiden

Guncannon Pilot

Originally a cynical civilian reluctant to fight, Kai develops from a sarcastic outsider into a dependable Guncannon pilot and trusted White Base crew member.

Hayato Kobayashi

Guntank Pilot

Amuro's childhood friend who pilots the Guntank artillery unit. Though quiet and often overshadowed, his determination and loyalty make him essential to White Base's defense.

Mirai Yashima

White Base Helmsman

From a wealthy family, Mirai proves calm and dependable under pressure as White Base's pilot. She becomes Bright's trusted partner, offering stability amidst chaos.

Ryu Jose

Core Fighter Pilot

A brave Federation officer who becomes a mentor figure for Amuro and the crew. Pilots the Core Fighter and provides steadying influence until his tragic sacrifice.

Fraw Bow

Civilian Caretaker

Amuro's childhood friend who takes responsibility for caring for war orphans aboard White Base. Though not a soldier, her compassion provides emotional balance for the crew.

Haro

AI Robot

Amuro’s small spherical companion—rolls around White Base entertaining the orphans and offering simple help, a cheerful reminder of ordinary life amid war.

Principality of Zeon

Char Aznable

Red Comet Ace Pilot

The masked "Red Comet" of Zeon, driven by personal vengeance against the Zabi family. His rivalry with Amuro defines the series' central conflict between duty and ideology.

Gihren Zabi

Supreme Commander

The eldest Zabi son and ruthless leader whose authoritarian rule drives Zeon's war against the Federation. His dictatorial ambition and rhetoric shape Zeon's militaristic ideology.

Dozle Zabi

Space Fleet Commander

Known for his large stature and surprisingly compassionate nature, Dozle commands Zeon's space fleets. Despite family loyalty, he shows genuine care for subordinates.

Garma Zabi

Earth Forces Commander

The youngest Zabi sibling who leads Zeon's Earth operations. His manipulation by Char becomes a pivotal turning point that reshapes the war's trajectory.

Kycilia Zabi

Intelligence Commander

The only Zabi daughter, sharp and calculating in her ambitions. She schemes against her brothers while seeking to advance her own influence within Zeon's power structure.

Degwin Sodo Zabi

Sovereign of Zeon

The Zabi patriarch who initially sought independence but gradually lost control as his son Gihren pushed the war to extremes. His conflicts with his children reveal Zeon's fractures.

M'Quve

Base Commander

Colonel in Zeon's Assault Mobile Force who commanded mining operations in occupied Eastern Europe during the One Year War.

Ramba Ral

Gouf Pilot

A veteran soldier who commands respect from allies and enemies alike. Unlike many Zeon officers, he values honor and fair combat, making him a sympathetic antagonist.

Crowley Hamon

Squad Leader

Ramba Ral's devoted companion who assumes command after his death and leads a final, tragic assault. Her actions underscore the loyalty and sacrifice of Zeon soldiers.

Lalah Sune

Elmeth Pilot

A pivotal Newtype whose bond with Amuro reframes the conflict; her fate defines the rivalry with Char.

Mobile Suits

Earth Federation

RX-78-2 Gundam

Earth Federation prototype mobile suit featuring the Core Block (FF-X7) System and a learning computer, designed for ground and space combat.

RX-77 Guncannon

A red artillery-type MS developed by the Earth Federal Army for medium-range support.

RX-75 Guntank

A navy blue tank type MS developed by the Earth Federation Army for long-range support.

G-Fighter

A versatile support craft that can combine with the Gundam and Core-Fighter to form multiple combat configurations.

RGM-79 GM

The Earth Federation’s first mass-produced Mobile Suit for close-range ground combat.

FF-X7 Core Fighter

A compact, high-speed craft that serves as the cockpit “core block” for RX-78-2 and includes a learning computer.

RB-79 Ball

A converted civilian work pod repurposed as a low-cost space-combat unit. Mounting a 180 mm recoilless cannon, thrusters, and manipulator arms.

Principality of Zeon

MS-06 Zaku II

The workhorse of Zeon’s army. Cheap, reliable, and versatile.

MS-07B Gouf

Designed for ground combat, the Gouf introduced the heat sword and whip into mobile suit combat.

MS-14 Gelgoog

Zeon’s late-war high-performance mobile suit. Comparable to the Gundam in ability.

MS-09 Dom

A heavy ground unit with high mobility thanks to hover jet engines. Famously used by the Black Tri-Stars.

MS-06S Zaku II

A high-performance version of the Zaku II customized for Char Aznable. It’s faster and deadlier than the standard type.

MAN-08 Elmeth

Prototype Newtype-use Space Mobile Armor piloted by Lalah Sune.

MSM-03 Gogg

Zeon’s amphibious assault suit. A heavily armored, water-jet–propelled bruiser built for underwater raids.

MA-08 Big Zam

Prototype Anti-Fortress Heavy Assault Space Mobile Armor.
