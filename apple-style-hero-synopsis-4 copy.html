Storyline

Original Series 1979

Set in Universal Century 0079, the series throws a teenage civilian, Amuro Ray, into the deep end when a surprise attack on his space colony destroys his home and forces him to pilot the Earth Federation's new prototype mecha, the RX-78-2 Gundam, just to survive.


What follows is a gritty space-war drama aboard the warship White Base, populated by a rag-tag crew of civilians and soldiers fleeing Zeon forces, navigating political intrigue, shifting loyalties, and the brutal costs of war.

Nations

Two opposing forces locked in the devastating conflict known as the One Year War

Earth Federation

The Earth Federation is the governing body of Earth and its space colonies. In the One Year War, they represent the established authority fighting against Zeon's bid for independence.


The Federation fields advanced warships, experimental mobile suits like the Gundam, and relies on the White Base and its young crew to carry critical hopes for survival.

Principality of Zeon

The Principality of Zeon is a breakaway nation of space colonies that declared independence from the Earth Federation. Led by the ruling Zabi family, Zeon launched the One Year War seeking dominance over Earth and space.


Known for their powerful mobile suits and charismatic leaders, Zeon characters often embody ambition, ideology, and the costs of militarism.
